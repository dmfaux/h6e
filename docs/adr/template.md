# NNNN. Short decision title

- **Status:** Proposed
- **Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders:** names or roles

## Context

What is the situation that forces a decision? What constraints apply (technical, legal, organisational)? Keep this factual. State the problem, not the answer. If this decision is driven by a regulatory or compliance constraint, say so explicitly here.

## Decision

What we are doing, stated plainly and in the active voice. One decision per ADR. If you are tempted to write "and also", that is probably a second ADR.

## Consequences

What this commits us to, good and bad. Include the costs and the things this makes harder, not just the benefits. Note anything a future change would have to deal with.

## Alternatives considered

What else we looked at and why we did not choose it. This is the part future readers value most, because it stops the same options being relitigated later.
